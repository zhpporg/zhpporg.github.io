Endless Play—and Possibilities!—with Dough

December 16, 2019
Making play dough is one of our favorite activities at Zion’s Hill Preschool Program … for children and teachers alike! But did you know that the original Play-Doh® was first made as a wallpaper cleaner in the 1930s? Upon learning that young children found modeling clay too difficult to manipulate, the manufacturer sent some of the pliable cleaning material to local schools in Cincinnati. Word quickly spread: the product was showcased at an education convention in 1955, and a year later, Play-Doh® was born.

About Us

Zion's Hill Preschool is located in Wilton, Connecticut. Our teachers have degrees in early childhood education and development, and the Board is controlled by parents.

In partnership with family and community, we provide a secure, developmentally appropriate, and nurturing setting promoting each child’s unique ability to reach their social, intellectual, and academic potential.

Zion’s Hill Preschool Program admits students of any race, color, religion and national or ethnic origin.
