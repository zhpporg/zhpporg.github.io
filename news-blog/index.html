About Us

Zion's Hill Preschool is located in Wilton, Connecticut. Our teachers have degrees in early childhood education and development, and the Board is controlled by parents.

In partnership with family and community, we provide a secure, developmentally appropriate, and nurturing setting promoting each child’s unique ability to reach their social, intellectual, and academic potential.

Zion’s Hill Preschool Program admits students of any race, color, religion and national or ethnic origin.
